Knuckle Taser Version 1

Version 1 of the taser uses a 9v battery going into a Boost converter, which turn the 9volts into 40,000 volts. It has two nails out of the topp which the electricity arcs form.

Knuckle Taser Version 2

Version two is the same design but it has a piece of metal acting as an external ground.
